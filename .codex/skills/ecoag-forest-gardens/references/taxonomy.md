  - Best sources:
    - `Ortiz-et-al-2020-forests-agroforestry-giahs-review.pdf`
    - `Castro-et-al-2025-temperate-food-forests-scoping-review.pdf`
    - `Castro-et-al-2018-temperate-agroforestry-forest-gardens.pdf`

- Temperate alley cropping and black walnut system design
  - Best sources:
    - `Bishop-et-al-2023-eastern-black-walnut-alley-cropping-review.pdf`
    - `Jose-2019-agroforestry-and-biodiversity.pdf`
  - Use for `Juglans nigra`, cultivar effects, phenology, light infiltration, interspecific competition, winter-grain complementarity, and U.S.-relevant alley systems.

## Question Routing

- "How should I lay out a forest garden, place layers, or reason about canopy/shade structure?"
  - Start with `references/forest-garden-layout.md`.
  - Then use `Castro-et-al-2025-temperate-food-forests-scoping-review.pdf` for temperate multistrata framing and establishment-phase constraints.
  - Add `Jose-2019-agroforestry-and-biodiversity.pdf` if the question depends on habitat heterogeneity, microclimate, or biodiversity mechanisms.

- "What is a temperate food forest / forest garden, and what does the literature actually support?"
  - Start with `Castro-et-al-2025-temperate-food-forests-scoping-review.pdf`.
  - Then use `Castro-et-al-2018-temperate-agroforestry-forest-gardens.pdf` for older temperate framing and forest-garden language.

- "How do food forests affect biodiversity, habitat, soil life, or ecological resilience?"
  - Start with `Jose-2019-agroforestry-and-biodiversity.pdf`.
  - Add `Ortiz-et-al-2020-forests-agroforestry-giahs-review.pdf` if the question includes landscape resilience, water regulation, or heritage systems.

- "How should a perennial multistrata system be established or maintained?"
  - Start with `Castro-et-al-2025-temperate-food-forests-scoping-review.pdf`.
  - Use `Castro-et-al-2018-temperate-agroforestry-forest-gardens.pdf` for temperate design rationale.
  - Flag that the local literature is stronger on framing and benefits than on mature-system maintenance protocols.

- "Can a food forest meaningfully support household food security or livelihoods?"
  - Start with `Galhena-et-al-2013-home-gardens-food-security.pdf`.
  - Add `Castro-et-al-2025-temperate-food-forests-scoping-review.pdf` if the user specifically wants temperate food-forest context.

- "How should Indigenous knowledge or traditional systems inform forest-garden design?"
  - Start with `Castro-et-al-2025-temperate-food-forests-scoping-review.pdf` for the research agenda and need for better integration.
  - Add `Ortiz-et-al-2020-forests-agroforestry-giahs-review.pdf` for traditional management and heritage-system functions.
  - Add `Castro-et-al-2018-temperate-agroforestry-forest-gardens.pdf` for people-based ethics and temperate cultural framing.

- "What does the local literature say about black walnut, walnut alleys, or tree-crop competition?"
  - Go directly to `Bishop-et-al-2023-eastern-black-walnut-alley-cropping-review.pdf`.
  - Use `Jose-2019-agroforestry-and-biodiversity.pdf` only for broader biodiversity context around alley systems.

- "Are forest gardens productive, mature, harvestable, or economically viable?"
  - Start with `Castro-et-al-2025-temperate-food-forests-scoping-review.pdf`.
  - Treat this as a weak-evidence area locally: the review explicitly identifies mature food forests, harvesting practices, and revenue models as research gaps.

- "How do traditional agroforestry systems handle water, slope, or landscape protection?"
  - Go to `Ortiz-et-al-2020-forests-agroforestry-giahs-review.pdf`.

## Source Inventory

- `references/forest-garden-layout.md`
  - Structured internal reference for layout reasoning. Best first stop for vertical layers, spacing logic, shade/use niches, and plant placement by site traits.

- `Castro-et-al-2025-temperate-food-forests-scoping-review.pdf`
  - Most current overview in this folder. Best entry point for temperate food-forest benefits, challenges, establishment phases, and stated research gaps.

- `Castro-et-al-2018-temperate-agroforestry-forest-gardens.pdf`
  - Best conceptual bridge between forest-garden, temperate agroforestry, perennial polyculture, and community-based / ethics-centered framing.

- `Jose-2019-agroforestry-and-biodiversity.pdf`
  - Best evidence base here for biodiversity mechanisms: floral, faunal, soil microbial, microclimate, organic matter, and habitat effects.

- `Ortiz-et-al-2020-forests-agroforestry-giahs-review.pdf`
  - Best for traditional management, heritage-system logic, hydrogeological protection, water regulation, and biodiversity maintenance in longstanding systems.

- `Galhena-et-al-2013-home-gardens-food-security.pdf`
  - Useful analog literature for multistorey household systems, food security, nutrition, livelihood, and practical constraints; less direct for temperate agroforestry design.

- `Bishop-et-al-2023-eastern-black-walnut-alley-cropping-review.pdf`
  - Narrow but high-value source for eastern North American alley cropping, especially cultivar-dependent walnut traits, phenology, light, and competition.

## Weak Evidence / Unresolved Gaps

- Mature temperate food forests are underrepresented relative to newly established systems.
- Harvesting practices and revenue models are explicitly thin in the local temperate food-forest review.
- Southern Hemisphere temperate evidence is weak in this folder.
- Indigenous knowledge is recognized as important, but operational integration into temperate food-forest practice remains underdeveloped in the local literature.
- This folder is strong on framing, biodiversity rationale, and system value, but weaker on species-by-species design prescriptions, management calendars, and quantified yield planning.
