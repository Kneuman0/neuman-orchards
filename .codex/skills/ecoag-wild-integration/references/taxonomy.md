## Major Themes

- Wild-to-managed transfer
  - Home-garden transplantation, tolerated volunteers, domestication gradients, encounter-driven adoption.
  - Start with `Hong-Zimmerer-2022-useful-plants-wild-to-home-gardens.md`, then `Polyzos-et-al-2025-wild-edible-plants-valorization-review.pdf`.
- Edible weed nutrition and safety
  - Nutrient density, toxicology, heavy metals, rinsing, urban harvest caution.
  - Start with `Stark-et-al-2019-open-source-food-wild-edible-greens.pdf`; add `Russo-et-al-2017-edible-green-infrastructure-review.md` for urban design tradeoffs.
- Cultivation and valorization of wild edibles
  - Propagation, germination, cropping systems, salinity/drought tolerance, niche crop development, phytoremediation.
  - Start with `Polyzos-et-al-2025-wild-edible-plants-valorization-review.pdf`; add `Kumar-et-al-2025-wild-edible-plants-global-overview.pdf` for broader food-system framing.
- Traditional use and food culture
  - Species use categories, edible parts, culinary persistence or decline, local specialties, knowledge erosion.
  - Start with `Pardo-de-Santayana-et-al-2007-wild-edible-plants-iberian-peninsula.pdf` and `Luczaj-et-al-2012-wild-food-plant-use-in-21st-century-europe.md`.
- Urban edible landscapes and green infrastructure
  - Provisioning services, disservices, contamination, designed edible landscapes.
  - Start with `Russo-et-al-2017-edible-green-infrastructure-review.md`; add `Stark-et-al-2019-open-source-food-wild-edible-greens.pdf` for measured tissue/soil evidence.
- Food security, conservation, and sustainable use
  - Dietary diversity, resilience, overharvest risk, conservation, ethnomedical and market framing.
  - Start with `Kumar-et-al-2025-wild-edible-plants-global-overview.pdf`.
