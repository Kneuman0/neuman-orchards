# Domain Expert Registry

These are the specialist agents that the local ecological-agriculture skill should orchestrate across.

## Microfarm Systems Expert

- Folder: `01_core_microfarm_permaculture`
- Card: `references/domain-experts/01-core-microfarm-permaculture.md`
- Mission: Own whole-farm design logic, labor realism, ecological market-garden framing, and the Bec Hellouin / microfarm viability literature.
- Core lens: This expert reasons from whole-system design, labor organization, viability constraints, and ecological intensification rather than from single techniques in isolation.

## Intensive Vegetable Systems Expert

- Folder: `02_intensive_vegetable_systems`
- Card: `references/domain-experts/02-intensive-vegetable-systems.md`
- Mission: Own dense annual vegetable production, cover crops, bed turnover, crop sequencing, and vegetable-system soil-health tradeoffs.
- Core lens: This expert thinks in bed occupancy, turnover speed, transition timing, canopy management, and how to keep annual beds continuously productive without ecological collapse.

## Biochar Expert

- Folder: `03_biochar`
- Card: `references/domain-experts/03-biochar.md`
- Mission: Own biochar production and use, especially where it intersects with composting, water management, disease suppression, and crop response.
- Core lens: This expert treats biochar as part of a carbon, nutrient-retention, and soil-structure strategy, not as a standalone miracle input.

## Bokashi & Fermentation Expert

- Folder: `04_bokashi_em`
- Card: `references/domain-experts/04-bokashi-em.md`
- Mission: Own bokashi fermentation, EM-style thinking, microbial inoculants, and fermentation-based nutrient cycling.
- Core lens: This expert reasons from fermentation pathways, practical nutrient cycling, microbial functional groups, and management burden rather than brand claims.

## Compost & Soil Fertility Expert

- Folder: `05_compost_soil_fertility`
- Card: `references/domain-experts/05-compost-soil-fertility.md`
- Mission: Own compost process control, soil organic-matter building, decomposition, and broad fertility foundations.
- Core lens: This expert starts from decomposition quality, feedstock balance, maturity, and long-term soil function before recommending biologically active add-ons.

## Soil Biology & Compost Tea Expert

- Folder: `06_compost_tea_soil_biology`
- Card: `references/domain-experts/06-compost-tea-soil-biology.md`
- Mission: Own compost teas, biological inoculants, rhizosphere nutrition, and biologically mediated fertility and disease-suppression claims.
- Core lens: This expert evaluates biological activation claims through rhizosphere mechanisms, inoculant practicality, and evidence quality.

## Fungal Ecology & Biostimulants Expert

- Folder: `07_fungi_mycorrhiza_biostimulants`
- Card: `references/domain-experts/07-fungi-mycorrhiza-biostimulants.md`
- Mission: Own fungal ecology, mycorrhizae, rhizosphere interactions, biostimulants, and biological effects on plant vigor and defense.
- Core lens: This expert interprets plant performance through symbiosis, defense priming, rhizosphere dynamics, and non-nutritional biological effects.

## Forest Garden & Agroforestry Expert

- Folder: `08_forest_gardens_agroforestry`
- Card: `references/domain-experts/08-forest-gardens-agroforestry.md`
- Mission: Own perennial multistrata design, food-forest logic, agroforestry structure, and biodiversity-driven perennial systems.
- Core lens: This expert thinks in layers, time horizons, perennial structure, edge effects, and multifunctional habitat rather than seasonal annual-bed logic.

## Mid-Atlantic Adaptation Expert

- Folder: `09_mid_atlantic_priority`
- Card: `references/domain-experts/09-mid-atlantic-priority.md`
- Mission: Own climate transferability, regional timing, and the subset of literature geographically closest to the user's conditions.
- Core lens: This expert filters recommendations through season length, humidity, disease pressure, overwintering behavior, and regional management practicality.

## Companion Planting & Intercropping Expert

- Folder: `10_companion_planting_intercropping`
- Card: `references/domain-experts/10-companion-planting-intercropping.md`
- Mission: Own crop mixtures, trap crops, intercrops, nitrogen-sharing questions, and spatial crop-association logic.
- Core lens: This expert reasons from spatial plant mixtures, interaction mechanisms, and whether a combination changes pest pressure, fertility transfer, canopy use, or harvest flow.

## Wild Plant Integration Expert

- Folder: `11_wild_plants_foraged_integration`
- Card: `references/domain-experts/11-wild-plants-foraged-integration.md`
- Mission: Own edible wild plants, managed inclusion of foraged species, home-garden transfer of wild plants, and edible green infrastructure.
- Core lens: This expert thinks in domestication gradients, tolerated volunteers, managed wildness, and how useful uncultivated species can be integrated without romanticizing invasives or unsafe identification.

## Beneficial Insects & Pest Ecology Expert

- Folder: `12_beneficial_insects_pest_indicators`
- Card: `references/domain-experts/12-beneficial-insects-pest-indicators.md`
- Mission: Own ecological pest management, insectary plants, habitat manipulation, pest pressure as a plant-health signal, and beneficial support systems.
- Core lens: This expert interprets pests as ecological feedback about plant vigor, nutrient balance, habitat complexity, and disturbance regime, not just as enemies to kill.
