## Indexed Resources

- `Felix-et-al-2023-agroecological-microfarm-prototype.pdf` - Co-Design and Experimentation of a Prototype of Agroecological Micro-Farm Meeting the Objectives Set by Climate-Smart Agriculture (2023). Recent experimental microfarm paper with explicit design criteria, useful for the technical side of small-scale agroecological farm planning.
- `Norris-Congreves-2018-intensive-vegetable-soil-health-review.pdf` - Alternative Management Practices Improve Soil Health Indices in Intensive Vegetable Cropping Systems: A Review (2018). High-value review for intensive vegetable production: amendments, cover crops, reduced tillage, soil biology, soil chemistry, and yield tradeoffs.
- `Navarrete-Dupre-Lamine-2014-crop-management-labour-marketing.url` - Crop management, labour organization, and marketing: three key issues for improving sustainability in organic vegetable farming (2014). Important market-garden systems paper on diversification, labor, and marketing. Saved as link because the direct PDF host blocked download.
- `Poeplau-et-al-2017-cover-crops-climate-review.pdf` - Using cover crops to mitigate and adapt to climate change. A review (2017). Cover crops are foundational to ecological farming; this review supports crop-rotation, soil protection, and adaptive management decisions.
