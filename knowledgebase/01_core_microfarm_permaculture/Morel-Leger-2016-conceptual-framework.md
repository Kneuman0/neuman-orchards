# A conceptual framework for alternative farmers' strategic choices: the case of French organic market gardening microfarms

- Authors: Kevin Morel and Francois Leger
- Year: 2016
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.1080/21683565.2016.1140695
- URL: https://orgprints.org/id/eprint/40495/

## Summary note

Multiple-case study of 14 French organic microfarms. Focuses on strategic decision making, tradeoffs, and viability beyond profit maximization alone.
