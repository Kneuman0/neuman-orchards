# Edible green infrastructure: An approach and review of provisioning ecosystem services and disservices in urban environments

- Authors: Alessio Russo, Francisco J. Escobedo, Giuseppe T. Cirella, Stefan Zerbe
- Year: 2017
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.agee.2017.03.026
- URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.agee.2017.03.026
- Why selected: Review on incorporating edible plants into designed landscapes while accounting for provisioning services, contamination, and tradeoffs.

## Summary note

Systematic review of edible green infrastructure typologies, ecosystem services, disservices, and design implications for urban and peri-urban food landscapes.
